Our Philosophy

A Full Educational Journey

Early Years (Baby-Reception)

Focus on play-based learning, cognitive development, and strong social skills in a caring environment.

Core Grades (Grade 1-7)

Comprehensive curriculum covering Math, Science, Language Arts, and Technology, preparing students for secondary school.

Holistic Growth

Emphasis on character building, critical thinking, and co-curricular activities like sports and arts.

Our Class Structure

ECE

Early Childhood Education

Includes Baby Class, Nursery, and Reception. Focus is on sensory exploration and foundational learning.

  • Motor Skills Development
  • Early Numeracy & Literacy
  • Social Play
G1-3

Lower Primary

Grades 1, 2, and 3. Transition from play-based to structured learning with core subjects.

  • Reading Fluency
  • Basic Arithmetic
  • Introduction to Science
G4-5

Middle Primary

Grades 4 and 5. Developing independence, research skills, and deeper subject understanding.

  • Complex Problem Solving
  • History & Geography
  • Digital Literacy
G6-7

Upper Primary

Grades 6 and 7. Final preparation for secondary school, including exam techniques and advanced concepts.

  • Critical Analysis
  • Advanced STEM
  • Leadership Programs
